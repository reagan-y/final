The Organization Resource Guide (.ORG), 2023
.ORG was a collaborative project with fellow design students. The goal of the book was to bring awareness to various organizations that the general public may not be knowledgable of by doing our own research and putting it in a book for them.
By Number, 2023
By Number was developed in an effort to make a type of color-by-number that didn't already exist. To take away all the prescriptiveness of the act of coloring, individuals would develop their own color palettes to color an abstract illustration.
Mighty Green, 2023
Mighty Green is a local lawncare company in Alabama and is a part of the Turf Masters Brands Family. While working with them, I created a brand refresh that included an updated logo and some print media. Instructed to stay as true to the original branding as possible, I went in two creative directions, and created a brand guide for the chosen identity.

Women and Aging, 2023
Women and Aging is an article by Neha Kale and Isabella Moore. This original article was a photo essay documenting women's experiences with aging. This book uses this article and imagery of rotten fruit to satiricalize the objectification of women and comment on the societal views of aging women as inconsumable.

The Arsenal uses the format of a swatch book to display an archive of over 300 different art supplies in my personal collection.
